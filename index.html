Spooktacular Stories

As the moon casts an eerie glow, you find yourself on the threshold of a spine-tingling adventure that will test your courage and wit. The air is filled with an enchanting sense of anticipation, for Halloween night has arrived, and with it, the promise of mysterious encounters and supernatural wonders. Join us on this interactive Halloween journey, where you'll make choices that will determine your fate in a world where the line between the living and the spectral realms is blurred. From haunted mansions to ancient cemeteries, your choices will shape your destiny in a story where ghouls, ghosts, and otherworldly beings await your decision. So, step into the shadows, and let your Halloween tale begin. Will you trick, or will you treat? The choice is yours.

How to play

  1. Enter your username (not all stories have usernames)
  2. Click the start button
  3. Choose your story
  4. Read your dialogue and make a choice
  5. Read your choice, the result of your choice, a new scene will then load
  6. keep making choices till the end screen
  7. Anytime you want to end the story click the home button at the top of the screen, this will take you back to the start screen
